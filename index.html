Full Stack in Progress

Currently on a journey to master full-stack development, focusing on both the front-end and back-end technologies. The goal is to build dynamic, interactive, and scalable web applications by gaining expertise in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React, and Node.js). With each project, there's a focus on learning how to efficiently manage data, create seamless user interfaces, and implement robust back-end systems. The process involves hands-on experience, problem-solving, and continuously refining skills to keep up with the evolving tech landscape

Projects

Project 1

This website is for a South Indian restaurant offering traditional dishes such as Appam, Vada, and Pesarattu Dosa. It emphasizes the use of organic masalas to enhance flavor and authenticity. The site highlights a cultural experience, showcasing a decor inspired by South Indian heritage, featuring elements like wooden furniture, brass lamps, and the soothing ambiance of classical Carnatic music. The design is simple yet effective, with easy navigation, clear product descriptions, and a focus on the restaurant's welcoming atmosphere.
